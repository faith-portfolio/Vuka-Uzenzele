WHO WE ARE

Vuka Uzenzele Cable Jointers Specialist is a 100% black-owned company, founded by John Risimati Ngobeni in 2007. We are experts in wide range of electrical work including cable network installation. We pride ourselves on high quality workmanship, safety and we respond on time to meet client expectation.

Vuka Uzenzele has concentration on providing competitively priced quality products and this philosophy has been a major contribution to its growth. Situated in the Gauteng Region we have undertaken major projects throughout the province related to cables. Our sound knowledge of the Electrical Industry allows us to be hands on in every aspect of the job from the beginning to the end.
